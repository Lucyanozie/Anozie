Travel Log: The Maldives

The Maldives, an archipelago of 26 atolls, is famous for its crystal-clear waters, white sandy beaches, and vibrant marine life. My recent trip to the Maldives was a peaceful escape into paradise, with plenty of opportunities for snorkeling, relaxing on the beach, and enjoying luxury overwater villas.

Waking up to stunning ocean views in the Maldives.

Things to Do

  1. Snorkel in the coral reefs

  4. Take a sunset dolphin cruise

Travel Tip

Tip: Book your excursions in advance to get the best spots and avoid last-minute availability issues.

One of my favorite moments was snorkeling with sea turtles – an unforgettable experience!

Top Attractions



Don’t miss the iconic Maafushi Island or a private island escape in the Maldives! The crystal-clear lagoons and vibrant coral reefs make these must-see spots.
