Fruit Salad

Fruit Salad is a quick and healthy mix of fresh fruits, drizzled with a simple honey-lemon dressing.

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Apples2, diced
Bananas2, sliced
Strawberries1 cup, halved
Grapes1 cup, halved
Honey2 tbsp
Lemon juice1 tbsp

Instructions

  1. Combine all fruits in a large bowl.
  2. Mix honey and lemon juice, and drizzle over the fruits.
  3. Toss gently and serve immediately.

Finished Dish

Enjoy your fresh and colorful fruit salad!
